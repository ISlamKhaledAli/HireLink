About the Role

We are looking for a Senior Product Designer to join our core product team. You will be responsible for defining the future of recruitment software, creating seamless experiences for both recruiters and job seekers globally. You'll work closely with product managers and engineers to bring complex systems to life with elegance and simplicity.

What You'll Do

  • Own the end-to-end design process, from research and ideation to final high-fidelity mocks.
  • Lead design workshops and sprints to solve complex product problems.
  • Collaborate with our design systems team to ensure consistency across our platform.
  • Conduct user research and usability testing to validate design decisions.
  • Mentor junior designers and contribute to our thriving design culture.
